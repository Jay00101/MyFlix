Common Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send you an email with instructions to create a new password.

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Why can't I access certain content?

Content availability varies by region due to licensing agreements. If you're traveling, you may notice different content selections.

Some content may also be restricted based on your profile's maturity settings. Check your account settings to adjust these restrictions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by visiting your Account page and selecting "Cancel Membership."

Your membership will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you can restart your membership at any time.
