Getting Started with MyFlix

How do I sign up for MyFlix?

Signing up for MyFlix is easy:

  1. Go to www.myflix.com/signup
  2. Choose your preferred plan (Basic, Standard, or Premium)
  3. Create your account by entering your email and creating a password
  4. Enter your payment information
  5. Start watching! You can stream immediately after signup
What devices can I use to watch MyFlix?

MyFlix is available on a wide variety of devices including:

  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, etc.)
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Apple TV, Chromecast, Fire TV)
  • Game consoles (PlayStation, Xbox)
  • Mobile devices (iOS, Android)
  • Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
  • Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
How many devices can stream simultaneously?

The number of simultaneous streams depends on your subscription plan:

  • Basic: 1 device at a time
  • Standard: 2 devices at a time
  • Premium: 4 devices at a time

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.

Troubleshooting

Why is my video buffering or quality poor?

If you're experiencing buffering or poor video quality, try these steps:

  1. Check your internet speed: Run our speed test to ensure you meet the minimum requirements (3.0 Mbps for SD, 5.0 Mbps for HD, 25 Mbps for Ultra HD)
  2. Reduce other network activity: Pause downloads or other streaming activities on your network
  3. Restart your device: Power cycle your streaming device and router
  4. Adjust playback settings: In your account settings, try lowering the data usage preference
  5. Try a wired connection: If possible, connect your device directly to your router with an Ethernet cable
I can't sign in to my account. What should I do?

If you're having trouble signing in:

  1. Make sure you're using the correct email address associated with your MyFlix account
  2. Check that your password is entered correctly (passwords are case-sensitive)
  3. Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  4. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again
  5. If you're still having issues, contact our support team
Why is MyFlix not working on my device?

If MyFlix isn't working on your device:

  • Check for updates: Make sure your MyFlix app and device software are up to date
  • Reinstall the app: Uninstall and then reinstall the MyFlix app
  • Check service status: Visit our Service Status page to see if there are any outages
  • Try another device: See if MyFlix works on a different device on the same network

If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device and the issue you're experiencing.

Account Help

How do I change my account email or password?

To update your account information:

  1. Sign in to your MyFlix account
  2. Go to "Account" from the profile dropdown menu
  3. Under "Membership & Billing", click "Update email" or "Change password"
  4. Enter your new information and save changes

Note: Changing your email will not transfer your viewing history or recommendations to the new email address.

How do I set up parental controls?

MyFlix offers robust parental controls:

  1. Go to "Account" and select "Parental Controls"
  2. Enter your account password to access settings
  3. Set maturity levels for profiles (Little Kids, Older Kids, Teens, Adults)
  4. Optionally set a PIN to restrict access to certain maturity levels
  5. You can also restrict specific titles regardless of maturity level

These settings apply to all profiles on your account except the main account holder.

Can I share my account with family members?

Yes! MyFlix allows you to create up to 5 profiles on a single account:

  • Each profile has its own viewing history and recommendations
  • You can set different maturity levels for each profile
  • Profiles can be customized with names and avatars

Note: While you can share your account with household members, simultaneous streaming is limited based on your subscription plan.

Billing & Payments

How do I update my payment method?

To update your payment information:

  1. Sign in to your MyFlix account
  2. Go to "Account" and select "Membership & Billing"
  3. Click "Update payment method"
  4. Enter your new payment details
  5. Click "Save" to confirm changes

Your new payment method will be charged for your next billing cycle.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

  1. Go to "Account" and select "Change plan"
  2. Choose your new plan (Basic, Standard, or Premium)
  3. Confirm the change

Note: Plan changes take effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount on your next billing date.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your MyFlix subscription:

  1. Sign in to your account
  2. Go to "Account" and select "Cancel Membership"
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. You can restart your membership at any time by signing in again.

Streaming Help

Why can't I find a specific movie or show?

If you can't find a particular title:

  • Check availability: Content varies by region due to licensing agreements
  • Search carefully: Try different search terms or browse relevant categories
  • Check your profile: Some content may be restricted based on profile maturity settings
  • Title may have been removed: We regularly update our catalog, and some titles may no longer be available

You can request titles through our content suggestion form.

How do I download shows to watch offline?

To download content on mobile devices:

  1. Open the MyFlix app on your iOS or Android device
  2. Find the show or movie you want to download
  3. Look for the download icon (downward arrow) on the details page
  4. Select download quality (higher quality takes more storage space)
  5. Wait for the download to complete

Note: Downloads expire after a period of time and some titles aren't available for download due to licensing restrictions.

How do I improve streaming quality?

For the best streaming experience:

  • Internet speed: Ensure you have sufficient bandwidth (5+ Mbps for HD)
  • Wired connection: Use Ethernet instead of Wi-Fi when possible
  • Close other apps: Reduce bandwidth usage from other applications
  • Adjust settings: In account settings, set playback to "High" quality
  • Restart router: Power cycle your network equipment
  • Update app: Ensure you're using the latest version of MyFlix
