1. Introduction

MyFlix ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our streaming service and related platforms (collectively, the "Service").

Important: By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, you should not use the Service.

1.1 Scope

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • On our website and mobile applications
  • In email, text, and other electronic communications
  • Through third-party applications you may use with our Service

1.2 Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

2. Data We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service:

Personal Information: Data that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly.

2.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our Service, you may provide:

  • Name, email address, and password
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment partners)
  • Profile information (avatar, preferences, etc.)
  • Communications with our support team

2.2 Automatic Collection

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data (content viewed, watch history, search queries)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for various business purposes:

3.1 Service Operation

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud
  • Personalize your experience (recommendations, profiles)

3.2 Communications

  • Respond to your requests and inquiries
  • Send service-related announcements
  • Provide marketing communications (where permitted)

3.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand usage patterns and preferences
  • Develop new features and content
  • Measure effectiveness of our Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information as described in Section 4 below.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We engage third parties to perform functions on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processing
  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Analytics and marketing services
  • Customer support platforms

These providers only access information needed to perform their functions.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests

4.3 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as a business asset.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

5.2 Preferences

You can manage certain preferences through your account settings:

  • Email and notification preferences
  • Profile information and viewing history
  • Parental controls for child profiles

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

6.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential: Necessary for service functionality
  • Preference: Remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics: Help us understand service usage
  • Advertising: Support personalized ads (where applicable)

6.2 Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect Service functionality.

We honor "Do Not Track" signals and do not track users across third-party websites for targeted advertising.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

7.1 Protection Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Employee training on data protection

7.2 Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your account credentials confidential
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Log out after using shared devices
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices.

8.1 Notification of Changes

We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (where required)
  • Displaying prominent notices within the Service

8.2 Your Acceptance

Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
